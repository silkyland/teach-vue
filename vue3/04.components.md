**4. Slots (30 ‡∏ô‡∏≤‡∏ó‡∏µ)**

  ```vue

  </div>
  ```

- **Named Slots**:  
  ```vue

  <MyComponent>
    <template v-slot:header>

    </template>
  </MyComponent>

  <!-- Child -->

  </div>
  ```

- **Scoped Slots**:  
  ```vue

  <slot :item="item"></slot>

  <!-- Parent -->
  <template v-slot:default="slotProps">
    {{ slotProps.item.name }}
  </template>
  ```
